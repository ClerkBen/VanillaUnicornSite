About Vanilla Unicorn

Welcome to Vanilla Unicorn — the hottest, most seductive strip club and bar in FiveM, where fantasy meets reality. We pride ourselves on creating an unforgettable experience filled with sultry performances, tantalizing drinks, and an atmosphere dripping with sensual charm.

Our talented dancers bring their unique style and passion to the stage every night, making sure every guest leaves a little breathless and craving more. Whether you’re here for a wild night out or a private VIP experience, we’ve got the perfect vibe for you.

Founded in 2024, Vanilla Unicorn has quickly become the premier destination for entertainment and nightlife in the FiveM world. We’re more than just a club — we’re a community. Join us to escape, indulge, and let your inhibitions run wild.

Our team is dedicated to ensuring your visit is safe, respectful, and endlessly enjoyable. We can’t wait to welcome you and show you what makes Vanilla Unicorn truly one of a kind.
